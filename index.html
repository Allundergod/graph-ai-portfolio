// The programme

About the course

An eight-week guided cohort by The GRAPH Courses on no-code AI tools for responsible task automation and research productivity.

Generative AI for Work & Research

Run by The GRAPH Courses, based at the University of Geneva. Eight weeks of live workshops, project reviews, and a capstone, anchored in academic rigour and practical, industry-relevant skills.

Weekly live workshops
Live help sessions
Private community forums
Personalised feedback
Capstone project coaching
Shareable certificate
MODULE 01

Websites, Documents & Presentations

How LLMs are trained, generating Markdown, LaTeX and HTML output, and building simple research websites with ChatGPT Canvas and Replit.

MODULE 02

AI-Automated Data Analysis

Working with ChatGPT Code Interpreter, Julius, and TheBricks to process datasets, build dashboards, and design spreadsheet automations for recurring analyses.

MODULE 03

GenAI for Research & Text Analysis

Literature reviews with Elicit and LiteRev, text classification, sentiment analysis, knowledge mapping, and automated summarisation workflows.

MODULE 04

Custom GPTs & Chatbots

LLM chat architecture, context windows, RAG systems, custom GPT design, and interactive chatbot building with Botpress.

MODULE 05

AI for Task Automation

Agent-based systems, email response automations, browser-control agents, and orchestrating app-to-app workflows responsibly.

MODULE 06

GenAI for Communication & Outreach

Research summaries for different audiences, scientific figures and diagrams, video abstracts, and prompt engineering for visualisation.

MODULE 07

Capstone Project & Showcase

Building a final AI solution with peers and instructors, peer feedback rounds, public Q&A, and a presentation that demonstrates responsible AI practice.
